ABOUT US

Our Story of Dedication & Growth

CFEL's story is rooted in dedication, craftsmanship, and growth. The journey began in the early 2000s, when our founder started working in glass and Thai aluminum on a small scale. With no formal structure at the time, the focus was simply on quality and hard work.

In 2015, this legacy was transformed into a registered company with proper trade licenses and official documentation. Since then, CFEL has steadily evolved from a fabrication-based business into a multi-disciplinary organization.

Today, we proudly provide end-to-end services in Fabrication, Building Construction, Road Construction, Development, and Design Consultancy.

Guided by experience and driven by innovation, CFEL continues to expand its ventures with a clear vision: to build with integrity, design with creativity, and develop with responsibility.

🎯 Our Mission

We prioritize the highest quality standards in every project, ensuring lasting value and sustainability. We strive to minimize environmental impact and promote sustainable construction practices.

Established in 2000, we're a leading civil engineering firm specializing in bridges, buildings, and railway projects. Our experienced team and commitment to quality ensure successful project completion across Bangladesh.

🔭 Our Vision

CFEL envisions itself as a leading force in shaping Bangladesh's infrastructure landscape, by pioneering innovative solutions, fostering a culture of excellence, and contributing to a sustainable future for the nation.

We aim to be recognized as the most trusted and innovative construction and engineering company in Bangladesh, delivering world-class quality with integrity.
